What It Does

Stuff Stash is built around four everyday inventory jobs.

Ask Where Something Is

Section titled “Ask Where Something Is”

Use normal household language:

Where is the fertilizer?

Stuff Stash searches the inventory you can access and answers in terms you recognize: garage shelf, office bin, medicine cabinet, wire rack.

Add Things Before The Moment Passes

Section titled “Add Things Before The Moment Passes”

Inventory gets stale when adding one thing takes too long. Stuff Stash keeps add flows close to the main workspace so you can capture the item, location, and photo while you still have the thing in your hand.

Search When You Want To Scan

Section titled “Search When You Want To Scan”

Voice is fast when you know what you want. Search is better when you want to compare close matches, inspect similar items, or avoid speaking.

Review Changes Before They Save

Section titled “Review Changes Before They Save”

Conversational updates should be fast without being reckless. When a voice or text command changes inventory state, Stuff Stash shows the planned action before it saves.
